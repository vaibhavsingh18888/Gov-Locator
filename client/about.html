What is GOVLocator?

GOVLocator is a free, citizen-focused web application designed to make it easy for people across India to locate and access government offices — from district collectors and municipal corporations to courts, revenue offices, and more.

Finding a government office can often be confusing. Office addresses change, phone numbers are hard to find, and working hours aren't always consistent. GOVLocator solves this by maintaining an up-to-date, searchable directory tailored for every citizen — accessible from any device, anywhere.

Whether you need to visit a collector's office, register a vehicle at an RTO, or file for a passport, GOV Locator shows you exactly where to go, how to get there, and when to visit.

How It Works

Find any government office in 3 simple steps

Use the search bar to find offices by name, city, or category — or filter by category with a single click.
